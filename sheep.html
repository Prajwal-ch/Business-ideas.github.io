🐑 Sheep Farming Business Guide

🌟 Why This Business?

Sheep farming is a low-maintenance, profitable livestock business ideal for rural areas. It offers income from meat, wool, and milk with strong market demand and long-term growth potential. 🧑‍🌾💰

🏁 PHASE 1: Getting Started

  • Choose good breeds like Nellore, Deccani, Merino
  • Build a small shed with basic fencing and clean water access
  • Feed a balanced mix of green and dry fodder
  • Vaccinate all sheep and schedule deworming regularly
  • Buy 10 sheep (male & female mix) to start

🚀 PHASE 2: Growth & Maintenance

  • Grow herd to 30+ sheep and improve shelter & feed systems
  • Use mineral mixtures and rotate grazing land regularly
  • Reinvest profit into breeding instead of buying new sheep
  • Sell manure and wool for extra earnings

🧠 PHASE 3: Advanced Expansion

  • Lease large grazing lands or cultivate fodder
  • Start processing unit for wool and sheep milk products
  • Brand and package mutton for local or online sales
  • Apply for NABARD subsidies and animal husbandry schemes

🔑 Success Tips

  • Pick disease-resistant breeds and keep their environment clean
  • Stick to a vaccination and deworming schedule
  • Monitor weight gain, feed efficiency, and mating cycles
  • Use manure for farming or sell it locally
  • Attend animal husbandry workshops and training
