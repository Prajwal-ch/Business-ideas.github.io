🚗 Company Car Ownership Business Guide

🔍 What Is It?

This business involves owning a fleet of vehicles and leasing or renting them to corporate clients. It offers steady income, demand from startups to MNCs, and opportunities to scale fast.

📊 Business Models

  • Corporate Leasing (monthly/yearly)
  • Executive & Guest Rentals
  • Employee Daily Commute Services
  • Ride-Hailing Fleet for Ola/Uber

🏁 Step-by-Step Startup Plan

📌 Phase 1: Registration

  • Register your company (Pvt Ltd or LLP)
  • Get commercial licenses, insurance, pollution, permits

📌 Phase 2: Buy Cars

  • Start with 2–5 fuel-efficient cars
  • Use commercial car loans (15–20% down payment)

📌 Phase 3: Setup & Operations

  • Hire drivers (with commercial license)
  • Install GPS, use fleet software for tracking

📌 Phase 4: Get Clients

  • Target corporates, IT firms, travel agencies
  • List on platforms and use LinkedIn outreach

🔑 Tips for Success

  • Keep vehicles clean, insured, and serviced regularly
  • Train drivers in professional conduct and safe driving
  • Use GPS tracking and record fuel usage
  • Offer corporate packages and added comfort
  • Use digital invoicing, CRM, and client tracking tools
