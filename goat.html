🐐 Goat Farming Business Guide

🌟 Why Start Goat Farming?

Goat farming is a low-investment and high-return agribusiness. Goats are easy to manage, require minimal space, and have high demand for meat and milk. Ideal for rural entrepreneurs looking to generate steady income.

🏁 PHASE 1: Initial Setup (₹70,000 – ₹1,50,000)

  • Select high-yield goat breeds like Boer, Sirohi, or Jamunapari
  • Construct a basic shed with proper ventilation and fencing
  • Buy 10 female goats + 1 male for breeding
  • Set up water supply and fodder storage
  • Consult a vet for vaccination schedule

🚀 PHASE 2: Operations & Growth

  • Breed goats and plan for 2-3 cycles per year
  • Sell kids at 6-8 months for meat or breeding
  • Explore goat milk and manure as income sources
  • Maintain detailed health & breeding records

🏗️ Design of Goat Shed

  • Raised wooden platform or cement floor
  • Separate area for pregnant and lactating goats
  • Feeding and watering areas with troughs
  • Proper drainage and air circulation

🔑 Success Tips

  • Choose disease-resistant and high-yield breeds
  • Provide balanced nutrition and fresh fodder
  • Follow strict hygiene and vaccination protocols
  • Market directly to meat shops, mandis, or online buyers
  • Join local goat farmer networks for guidance and support
